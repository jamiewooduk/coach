Roleplay Scenario: Difficult Performance Conversation

  • You are a manager who has set up a meeting with your direct report, Joe.
  • Joe has been struggling with time management and meeting deadlines.
  • Your goal is to use the meeting to provide constructive feedback and discuss ways to improve performance.

Key Points to Remember:

  • Be specific and use concrete examples
  • Focus on behaviors and outcomes, not personal characteristics
  • Listen actively and encourage a two-way dialogue
  • Collaborate on setting clear, actionable goals for improvement
  • Express confidence in the employee's ability to improve
